Fix bugs fast!

Slipspace groups related errors to help you fix the root cause of the issue. You'll never be overwhelmed by a deluge of trivial errors again.

Simple metrics

Track custom metrics such as query response time or the time taken for a distributed transaction to complete to make sure your app is always performant.

Keep your clients happy

You'll have all the information about problems with the application at your fingertips. Let the client know when the bug first occurred, how many times it surfaced, and how many users were affected at the drop of a hat.

Be proactive

Track bugs before your client notices them, let them know the bug was fixed before it becomes an issue.

Happy clients pay their bills on time

Each client gets their own status page. No more manual reports, surface information here in real time. You can even surface metrics so your clients know their SLAs are being hit.

Built to be sublicensed

Slipspace is completely brandable and can be hosted under your own domain. This allows you to sublicense our product to use within your existing support processes, all without your customers being aware that it's an external service.
